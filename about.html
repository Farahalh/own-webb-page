About me

Front-end developer from Gothenburg, Sweden

Hello, I am Farah Alhaddad, a skilled front-end developer based in Sweden. When I am not working, I enjoy spending quality time with my loved ones and exploring my passion for Bachata dancing.

For me, coding is a way of expressing my creativity and problem-solving skills. I find joy in tackling challenging tasks, such as creating landing pages or developing weather applications.

My professional journey started in the textile industry, where I studied to become a pattern maker. As a technical designer, my role was to assist the designer in bringing their vision to life by creating technical instructions and moulds for factories. This experience has given me a strong foundation in problem-solving and collaboration, which I have found to be invaluable in my current profession.

After exploring various career options, I decided to transition into front-end development. I enrolled in a program with www.shecodes.io, where I have gained valuable experience and knowledge that has helped me to grow as a developer.

Currently, I am focusing on completing my studies, seeking out new project ideas, and actively searching for my next opportunity to join a team and contribute my skills and experience as a front-end developer.
